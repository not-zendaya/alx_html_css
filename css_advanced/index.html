F.A.Q.

What is smile school?

Smile School is an online learning platform focused on personal development through video tutorials. It offers curated content from industry professionals to help users learn at their own pace. The platform is 100% online, allowing access from anywhere in the world. You can browse tutorials based on categories or recommendations. Each course includes video content, instructor bios, and progress tracking. All you need is a free membership to start accessing the materials.

Is Smile School really free?

Yes, Smile School offers free access to a wide range of courses for all registered users. You don’t need to provide any payment information to sign up. Free membership includes unlimited access to beginner-level tutorials. You can upgrade to a premium membership for advanced content and certifications. Even without paying, you'll benefit from high-quality videos and expert instruction. The goal is to make education accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Who are the mentors on Smile School?

The mentors are experienced professionals from various industries such as tech, design, and marketing. Each mentor is carefully selected based on their expertise and communication skills. Profiles include details about their work experience and past achievements. You'll get to watch them in action as they explain real-world concepts in simple terms. Mentors also share personal insights and tips that can’t be found in textbooks. This helps learners gain both theoretical knowledge and practical wisdom.

Do I receive a certificate after completing tutorials?

Yes, Smile School offers digital certificates for users who complete designated learning paths. These certificates can be downloaded and shared on LinkedIn or your resume. They serve as proof of effort and skill acquisition. Not all tutorials are certificate-eligible—only those marked as “Certified.” Completing quizzes and assignments within those tutorials is required. This ensures that learners actually understand the material before certification.
